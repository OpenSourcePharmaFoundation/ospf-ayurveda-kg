MEDICINAL PLANT DATABASE OF INDIA

Plants are employed in different medicinal systems and ethnic medicines. India has rich tradition of herbal medicines and it has outstanding contributions not only in form of Ayurveda & Siddha but also in the discovery of modern drugs and pharmacological research. Our medicinal plant database provides information on scientific name, family, vernacular name, medicinal uses, location of species and images of herbarium specimen. In first phase, a total of 1,915 species are enlisted and about 1,000 species will be added in next phase.

Note: This database is dynamic in nature with weekly updates, we have no archive version of the medicinal plant database of India, therefore, we advised users to personally store cited or used data.
